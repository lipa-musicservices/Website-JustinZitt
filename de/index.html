About

A musical language built on clarity, atmosphere, and spontaneity.

Justin Zitt is a pianist and composer based in Amsterdam. His music brings together lyrical writing, rhythmic precision, and an openness to the unpredictable energy of live performance. Whether working in solo settings, collaborative ensembles, or project-based formats, he is drawn to music that communicates directly while leaving space for nuance, texture, and movement.

As a performer, Justin is interested in the tension between structure and freedom: composed material that remains open enough to breathe, shift, and evolve in the moment. As a composer, he focuses on strong, memorable musical ideas, patient development, and a sonic world that balances intimacy with depth.

The result is a body of work shaped by attention to detail, sensitivity in ensemble playing, and a commitment to music that feels honest rather than decorative. That is the whole point, really.

Release

FRIGO

FRIGO is a release shaped by contrast: stillness and momentum, precision and openness, closeness and distance. The music unfolds through carefully formed compositions while staying connected to the energy of performance itself. Rather than treating composition and improvisation as opposites, FRIGO lets both coexist — structure becomes a frame for movement, and spontaneity becomes part of the form.

Concept A body of music built around atmosphere, clarity, and the tension between fixed material and live development.
Writing Compositions that stay melodic and focused while allowing space for shifting colors and unexpected turns.
Sound Acoustic intimacy, cinematic pacing, and a strong sense of detail in timing, texture, and ensemble balance.
Live Context Designed not only as a recorded statement, but also as material that can grow and change on stage.
